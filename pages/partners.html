Partners.

We are proud to collaborate with outstanding organizations that support the growth and development of Air and Space Force officers and Airmen.

Developing Future Enlisted Officers (DEFO)

DEFO is a Commanderâ€™s Program that resides within the CGOC and connects Airmen with prior-enlisted CGOs, guiding them through the commissioning process, providing shadowing opportunities within requested AFSCs (as feasible), commissioning package reviews, and monthly mentorship.

If your base is interested in starting a DEFO program or growing your program, please contact the below POCs and complete the DEFO Program Survey. You can also join the DEFO Facebook group.

Arnold Air Society (AAS)

Arnold Air Society is a professional, honorary, service organization comprised of Air Force and Space Force Cadets who are members of the Air Force Reserve Officer Training Corps (AFROTC) and United States Air Force Academy (USAFA). They are dedicated to their community and to becoming Air Force Officers. The DAFCGOC supports AAS by connecting cadets with Air and Space Force officers to ease their transition into Active Duty.

Military Officers Association of America (MOAA)

The MOAA Foundation (TMF, EIN 46-4219250), a 501(c)(3) charitable subsidiary of the Military Officers Association of America (MOAA), offers grants to MOAA, its councils and its chapters in support of career development and community outreach programs and services. These programs and services benefit more than 50,000 currently serving uniformed service members and veterans of all ranks, their families and their survivors each year.
