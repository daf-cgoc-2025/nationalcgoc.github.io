We are a member-based organization, driven by the strength and engagement of our community. We are looking out for you, and the more we grow, the more significant our impact.

WHO CAN JOIN MOAA? MOAA membership is open to ROTC and service academy cadets and midshipmen from all services in their junior and senior years, as well as commissioned and warrant officers (active duty, retired, former, Guard, and Reserve) from all uniformed services.

WHAT ARE EARNED BENEFITS? From basic pay to the GI bill, from health care to retirement (and much more), your earned benefits recognize your service and commitment by providing support for you and your family. These benefits often face threats during congressional budget reviews, and MOAA protects them through strong advocacy and legislative engagement.

JOIN MOAA TODAY Become a BASIC member for free at https://www.moaa.org/join to stay in the know for updates on military issues and benefit changes that affect you and those you will lead.
