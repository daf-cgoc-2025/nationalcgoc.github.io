Why Compete?

Annual Awards

Annual winners are eligible for the Air and Space Recognition Ribbon — a permanent decoration on your service record.

Quarterly Awards

Quarterly winners receive the official DAF CGOC Coin Package in recognition of their outstanding contributions.
